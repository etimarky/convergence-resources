# Reclassify Parts


In SmartFind, you are able to move a part from its existing category to a different category; this process is known as reclassifying a part.


 


First, select a random part from any category at any category level.


Review the root (the category where the part is located)


In this example, the part is located at the lowest category level. This part can be moved up to its parent category or be moved to the lowest level of any parent category. 


Click on Reclassify.


![](assets/image(1077).png)




 


Now you are able to see where the part its classified and what attributes pertain to that category. 


You are able to reclassify the part by clicking on any of the category levels.


![](assets/image(1082).png)




 


In this example, the part will be moved up to an upper category by clicking on Airflow.


The subcategories under Airflow are displayed below the category.


![](assets/image(1085).png)




 


By clicking on Fans, the subcategories under this category are displayed.


Then Fan Blades is selected.


Click on Ok to reclassify this part.


 


![](assets/image(1080).png)




 


The part has been reclassified. This can be reviewed in the Part Details.
