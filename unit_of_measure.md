**Unit of Measure**


 


Units of Measure vary for each type of numeric attribute. DFR offers commonly used units of measure with each Unit of Measure Type.  


 


Unit of Measure examples:


 Length   inches, millimeters, meters, feet


 Frequency  GHz, Hz, kHz, MHz


 


If an attribute value does not have a unit of measure then most likely the attribute type should not be numeric.
