**Resizing 
the Detail Grid**



The 
user can resize the detail grid, by clicking the *minimize* or *maximize* 
buttons:


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-d2f34nel28gg/DD-Resizing-the-Detail-Grid_files/image001.jpg)




**Example**: Minimize the lower 
grid.


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-d2f34nel28gg/DD-Resizing-the-Detail-Grid_files/image002.jpg)




**Example**: Maximize the lower 
grid.


 


*Note*: The user may also 
resize the panels manually by clicking the area between the grid, and dragging 
the controls.
