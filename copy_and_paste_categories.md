# Copy and Paste Categories

Copy\_and\_Paste\_Categories - Design For Retrieval (DFR) Help

## Copy and Paste Categories

To copy and paste a category, first find the category that you would like to copy.

&#x20;

![](http://userfiles-kb.s3.amazonaws.com/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/qu/3939/image.png)

&#x20;

Next, click on the three dots on the right of the category name. Then in the pop-up window click "Copy".

&#x20;

&#x20;

![](http://userfiles-kb.s3.amazonaws.com/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/qu/8682/image.png)

&#x20;

You will now see this icon next to the category name letting you know it has been copied.

&#x20;

![](http://userfiles-kb.s3.amazonaws.com/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/qu/8072/image.png)

&#x20;

Now click on another parent category where you would like to paste the category, and click the three dots to the right of the parent category and click paste.

&#x20;

![](http://userfiles-kb.s3.amazonaws.com/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/qu/6896/image.png)

&#x20;

You have now successfully copied and pasted a category to another category.
