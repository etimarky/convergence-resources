**Responsible Users**


 


Responsible Users lists the users who have access to the 
classification and assigns certain functions to the users.


 


·         
**Developers** are the individuals who are allowed to add, 
edit, update and copy nodes within the category. They may also submit the 
category for approval.


 


·         
**Approvers** are the only user who can approve a node. 
They may also add, edit, update, copy and submit for approval nodes within this 
category. In addition, they may also delete the nodes within this category and 
use the prune and graft functions to move a category and its children to 
another location in the classification system.  They can also create and 
change description templates.


 


·         
**Notification Recipient** lists the people who will be 
notified via email when changes are made to the status of a category. See 
Administrators guide on how to configure Notifications.
