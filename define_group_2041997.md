



Define\_Group - Design For Retrieval (DFR) Help



**Define Group**


 


To define a group:


 


- Press the **Define Group...** button. The **Define Attribute Groups** pop-up window will be displayed.
- Scroll down the list of existing groups and enter the new group **Name** and **Description** on the next available line.

The **Create Date** and **Modify Date** will be entered automatically when the group name is saved. 


- Press **OK** to save the new group.
- Press **Cancel** to clear the unsaved group name and description.
