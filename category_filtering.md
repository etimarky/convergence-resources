## Category Filtering

&#x20;

You can filter into categories in SmartFind if you are not in a leaf category. Browse to a parent category.&#x20;

&#x20;

In the category tree on the left, you can select the child categories to the parent category you are in.&#x20;

&#x20;

You can also sort the children's categories by relevance or by alphabetical order. You can do this by clicking the highlighted "Alpha Sort" or " Relevance Sort" icons. You will see the category tree change as you click the icons.&#x20;
