**Add an Attribute to a Group**


 


To add an attribute to a group:


 


- Select the attribute and press the [Edit](javascript:popuplink('hs2120.htm')) button.
- Select the **Group** tab.
- Press the **down arrow** to the right of the **Name** and **Description** table to display the available groups.
- Select the desired group and press the **Add** button. Note that the new group has been added to the group list for the current attribute.
- Press the **Save** button at the top of the Attribute Manager screen.
