# Add Attributes to a Display Tab

Add\_Attributes\_to\_a\_Display\_Tab - Design For Retrieval (DFR) Help

## Add Attributes to a Display Tab

&#x20;

1. First navigate to SmartClass

&#x20;

2. Next, find a leaf category you would like to work in. Click on an attribute as shown below.&#x20;

&#x20;

3. Click on the blue "Edit" button in the top right of the right hand panel that has popped up.&#x20;

&#x20;

4. Drop down the "Properties" menu and scroll down until you find "Display Tab". Enter the name of the tab you would like to have added.&#x20;

&#x20;

5. Click on the green "Save" button at the top right of your screen.&#x20;

&#x20;

&#x20;

&#x20;

&#x20;

![](https://userfiles-kb.s3.amazonaws.com/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/qu/5983/pic%20one.png)

&#x20;

&#x20;

6. You will now see the display tab in the item details page, with the one attribute that you added to it.&#x20;

&#x20;

![](https://userfiles-kb.s3.amazonaws.com/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/qu/9572/pic%202.png)
