# Add Attributes to a Display Tab


 


1. First navigate to SmartClass


 


2. Next, find a leaf category you would like to work in. Click on an attribute as shown below. 


 


3. Click on the blue "Edit" button in the top right of the right hand panel that has popped up. 


 


4. Drop down the "Properties" menu and scroll down until you find "Display Tab". Enter the name of the tab you would like to have added. 


 


5. Click on the green "Save" button at the top right of your screen. 


 


 


 


 


![](https://userfiles-kb.s3.amazonaws.com/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/qu/5983/pic%20one.png)




 


 


6. You will now see the display tab in the item details page, with the one attribute that you added to it.
