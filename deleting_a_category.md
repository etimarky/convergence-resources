**Deleting a Category**


 


**The category developer role  is required to delete a category.**


 


To delete a category:


 


- Select the category to be deleted

 


- Press the right mouse button to display the **Category Operations** drop down menu.

 


- Select **Delete Category**

 


The category will be deleted from the classification.
