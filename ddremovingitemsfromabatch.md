**Removing 
Items from a Batch**



To 
delete an item from a working batch, select the item, right click, and select 
“Delete Item From Working Batches”.


**![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/DD-Removing-Items-from-a-Batch_files/image001.jpg)**


**Example**: Deleting an item 
from a working batch.


 


After 
choosing “Delete Item from Working Batches…”, a dialog box appears to choose 
which batch to remove the item from.


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/DD-Removing-Items-from-a-Batch_files/image002.jpg)




**Example**: removing an item 
from a batch.


 


Choose 
the batch you wish the item to be removed from, and choose “Ok”.


 


After 
choosing OK, the item has been deleted from the batch.


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/DD-Removing-Items-from-a-Batch_files/image003.jpg)




**Example**: Item deleted from 
batch “new batch name”.


 


*Hint:* To delete multiple 
items from a batch, follow the instructions in the Manage Working Batches 
section below.
