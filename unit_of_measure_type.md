**Unit of Measure Type**


 


A Unit of Measure Type defines the attribute value and is expressed in a Unit of Measure. For example, an an attribute value of "6" may have a Unit of Measure Type of Pressure. Pressure can be expressed in Units of Measure of kpsi, Pa, psi, etc.
