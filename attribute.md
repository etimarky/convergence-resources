**Attribute**


 


 


An Attribute is a descriptor about an item. Each item is unique based on the values assigned to its attributes. 


 


Attributes are defined in the [Attribute Manager](hs2000.md) tool. Once added, they become part of the [Master Attribute List](javascript:popuplink('hs2751.htm')). 


 


Attributes are assigned to categories or terminal nodes in the Classification Manager tool using the [Edit Attributes...](javascript:popuplink('hs2320.htm')) operation.


 


Attributes assigned to categories may be passed down to each subordinate category based on the [Inheritance](javascript:popuplink('hs920.htm')) selected in the category definition. 


 


Attribute values may be loaded into the database in the [Item Loader](hs3000.md) tool, or they me added in the [Data Developer](hs4000.md) tool.
