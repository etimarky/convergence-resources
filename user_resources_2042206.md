**User Resources**


 


Titles for these users are assigned when the system is configured. Examples include:


 


- Enterprise Owner - This personmay have responsibility for a category as a product line manager, or it may be a group of individuals

 


- Content Expert - This should be someone who best understands the details of a product line and may be referred to for more information
