**Re-Classifying 
Items**



To 
re-classify items in Data Developer, you can drag the item from the main window 
into the desired category in the category tree.  In order to reclassify items, 
the item needs to be writeable; that is, it must be assigned to the current 
user.


 


For 
example, to reclassify the highlighted item, click it, and drag it over to the 
desired category.


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/DD-ReClassify_files/image001.jpg)




**Example**: Re-classifying an 
item into the “Document Information” category.


 


**Note**: If the item you are 
re-classifying has attributes that differ from the target category attributes, 
you will receiving a warning message similar to the one below:


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/DD-ReClassify_files/image002.png)




**Example:** Warning message 
indicating that the attributes for the item differs from the attributes for the 
target category.
