**Limiting 
Search to Previously Found Items**


 


Limiting 
a search to previously found items is useful when you want to search a number 
of items returned from a previous search.  In the *Searching by Item Number 
or Description* example, we found all Items that began with DDXB.  If we 
wanted to find all items with “-13” in them from the previous search results, 
we could search for:


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-d2f34nel28gg/DD-Limiting-Search-to-Previously-Found-Items_files/image001.jpg)




**Example**: Searching a subset 
of items for all items with “-13” in the name.


 


**Note**: Searching is 
limited to items within the selected category, as well as to the Batch, User, 
and Status selected.
