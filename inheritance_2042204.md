**Inheritance**


 


The classification hierarchy allows a user to 
assign characteristics at one category level and have those characteristics 
flow down along the branch to all subordinate categories. This characteristic 
is called "Inheritance" and is determined by a check box in the 
Category Maintenance Window.


 


Attributes, Approval and Copy Display Order 
settings may be inherited from the parent node.


 


A check in the **Inherit Attributes** box 
indicates that the node is to automatically include the attributes of its 
parent node.   


 


If the Inherit Attributes box is NOT checked, 
the current node and its subordinate nodes will not inherit the attributes of 
the parent and may be assigned different attributes than the parent node.


 


A check in the **Inherit Security** box 
indicates that the node is to automatically include the user access roles of 
its parent node.


 




A check in the **Copy Display Order** will inherit the Display Order of attributes to 
that of its parent node.
