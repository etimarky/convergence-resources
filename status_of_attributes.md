



Status\_of\_Attributes - Design For Retrieval (DFR) Help



**Status of Attributes**


 


- **New** - indicates a newly attribute that has had no status changes
- **In Work** - indicates an attribute that has been reviewed but now needs to be reworked by the developer
- **Pending** - indicates that an attribute has been submitted by the developer and is waiting for approval by the approver
- **Rework** - indicates that a submitted and approved attribute, or a rejected attribute, has been modified to In Work status for modification by the developer
- **Approved** - indicates that an attribute has been approved by the approver
- **Rejected** - indicates that a submitted and approved attributehas been rejected by the approver. This may indicate that circumstances have changed regarding this attribute, or that it is obsolete
