**Searching 
By Item Number or Description**


 


To 
search for an item by the item’s name or description, select the “Item 
Number/Description” tab and enter a search string.  The “Search String” can 
match using a wildcard (“%”). So, to find all items that begin with DDXB, you 
could enter DDXB% as your search string, and press search.  The wildcard is 
necessary unless you enter the complete number.


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/DD-Searching-By-Item-Number-or-Description_files/image001.jpg)




**Example**: Searching for all 
items that begin with *DDXB.*


 


**Note**: To do a search for 
all items, there is no need to search for “%”.  Just double click the category 
and all data will be returned or select the category and click Retrieve.
