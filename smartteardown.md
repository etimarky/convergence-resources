## SmartTeardown

The SmartTeardown module allows users to break down products into a product structure and compare different modules side by side (including images). This new CDS PIM module will not only allow service clients to store any competitive intelligence data they collect but make it a seamless process to deliver data to employees. Long gone are the days of finding the latest PowerPoint or Excel sheet. Stay up to date with dynamic data utilizing the power of CDS PIM Online!
