# Multi-Classify

MultiClassify - Design For Retrieval (DFR) Help

## Multi-Classify

&#x20;

If you would like to have an item be in multiple categories at the same time, you can Multi-Classify an item.&#x20;

&#x20;

Navigate to the item you would like to multi-classify in SmartFind.

In the top right of your screen, click the button with folder on it highlighted below.

&#x20;

![](.gitbook/assets/image_16.png)

&#x20;

&#x20;

Now in the pop-up window, drill down to the category you would like to add the item to.&#x20;

&#x20;

Click on the category and click confirm in the bottom right.&#x20;

&#x20;

![](.gitbook/assets/image_17.png)

&#x20;

&#x20;

Now you will see the categories that the item is classified in. You can click the red "x" next to the categories to de-classify the item from that category.&#x20;

&#x20;

![](.gitbook/assets/image_18.png)
