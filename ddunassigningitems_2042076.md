**Unassigning 
Items**


 


To unassign an item(s) in Data Developer, select the item(s), 
right click, and choose Unassign Item.


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-d2f34nel28gg/DD-Unassigning-Items_files/image001.jpg)




**Example**: Unassign 3 items.


 


Once the items have been unassigned, you’ll notice the Assigned 
User column is blank for those items.


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-d2f34nel28gg/DD-Unassigning-Items_files/image002.jpg)




**Example**: After unassign, the “Assigned User” field is blank.
