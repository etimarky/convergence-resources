**Resizing 
the grid**


The grid can be split horizontally or vertically. To move the 
splitter location, left click the splitter location, and drag it to the desired 
location.

**Editing 
Regional Cost/Usage Information**


 


Editing regional cost and usage information can be done on 
the main tab of item mapping. To assign values, click on a variant-year, and type 
the desired value. Edited cells will be highlighted green to indicate they have 
been changed.



**![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/IM-Grid-9-30_files/image005.jpg)**


**Example: Edited Cost/Usage data is shown in green.**



**Regional 
Item Detail Information**



Editing item detail information can be done on the main tab 
of item mapping. To assign values, click on a variant-year, and type the 
desired value.  Edited cells will be highlighted green to indicate they have 
been changed.


**![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/IM-Grid-9-30_files/image006.jpg)**

**Replacements**


Replacing one variant year with another can be accomplished 
by either dragging a variant year on top of another variant year, or by using 
the replacements tree in the bottom right corner of the screen.  To use the 
replace by tree, select the variant you want to be replaced, and then check the 
appropriate checkbox in the replacements tree view.


 


 


*For example: to replace the 2009 variant year for variant 
W10237493 with the 2010 variant year for variant W10237499:*


Step 1) click the button corresponding to the 2009 variant 
of the W10237493![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/IM-Grid-9-30_files/image007.jpg)




 


Step 2) check the checkbox in the replacement tree view


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/IM-Grid-9-30_files/image008.jpg)




**Example: The 2009 variant for W10237493 has now been 
replaced by the 2010 variant for W10237499.**



To remove replacements, uncheck the check box in the 
replacements tree view.


 


**Searching 
in the Tree View** 


With large maps, it is possible for the tree view to contain 
a large number of variants. This is where the search feature is used.  Typing 
text into the search box will highlight the nodes that match.  


 


![](/userfiles/13115/16830/ckfinder/images/import-cbg4qpn20hv5/IM-Grid-9-30_files/image009.jpg)




**Example: Typing 499 in the search box, will automatically 
expand, and highlight all nodes that that contain 499.**
