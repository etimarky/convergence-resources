# Smart Find



 

 


 


 


Welcome to the documentation for SmartFind, a module of Convergence PIM online software!


SmartFind is a powerful tool for searching and finding data within your organization. With its advanced search capabilities, you can easily locate the information you need, regardless of where it is stored.


Some key features of SmartFind include:


- Full-text search: SmartFind allows you to search for data across your data housed in Convergence PIM's database. This means you can find the information you need by searching for any word or phrase within the data.
- Customized search filters: You can use SmartFind's customizable search filters to narrow down your results and find the most relevant information. For example, you can search for data within a specific date range, or filter by data type or location.
- User-friendly interface: The SmartFind interface is designed to be easy to use, with a simple search bar and clear search results. This makes it easy for anyone in your organization to find the data they need quickly and efficiently.
- Advanced search capabilities: SmartFind includes advanced search capabilities, such as wildcard searches and Boolean operators, to help you find specific data more accurately.


Overall, SmartFind is a valuable tool for quickly and easily finding data within your organization. Whether you are looking for specific documents, data sets, or other information, SmartFind can help you locate it quickly and easily.
