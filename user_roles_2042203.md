



User\_Roles - Design For Retrieval (DFR) Help



**User 
Roles**


 


- **Category 
 Developers** are 
 the individuals who are allowed to add, edit, update and copy nodes 
 within the category. They may also submit the category for approval.

 


- **Category 
 Approvers** are 
 the only user who can approve a node. They may also add, edit, 
 update, copy and submit for approval nodes within this category. In 
 addition, they may also delete the nodes within this category and 
 use the prune and graft functions to move a category and its 
 children to another location in the classification system.  
 They can also create and change description templates.

 


- **Notification 
 Recipient** lists 
 the people who will be notified via email when changes are made to a 
 category. This function is not implemented yet.
